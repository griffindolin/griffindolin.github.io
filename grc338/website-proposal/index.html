Introduction

The Cuts Alley Shop

The Cuts Alley Shop is a traditional barbershop heavily inspired by speakeasy bars, located in the east bay. We strive to offer high-quality luxury barber services at a reasonable price. Our talented barbers can cut any type of hair and we welcome anyone with open arms, that is if you can find us!

Target audience

People looking for a hair cut from a traditional barber. Predominantly male crowd 25-60.

Their main goal on the website is usually booking appointments. Also for new patrons looking up the location of the shop. Finally learning more about the background of the business.

about

Born from the idea of 1920s style prohibition bars, The Cuts Alley Shop is speakeasy Esq and sits below a fully functioning restaurant and bar. Once inside you will feel welcome in a very traditional style barbershop. Our master barbers and stylists can offer traditional shaves, quality haircuts, and even hot towel treatments. Our goal is to have our customers leave with the haircut they actually wanted and an experience that they will remember. We want our barbershop to be integrated into the community and not only be a place to get your haircut but a place to talk and hang out as well.
